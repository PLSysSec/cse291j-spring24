**CSE 291-J: Blockchain Security** [*Stefan Savage*](https://cseweb.ucsd.edu/~savage/) and [*Deian Stefan*](https://cseweb.ucsd.edu/~dstefan/) About ============================================================== This course focuses on the security of blockchain technologies. Students will be introduced to many parts of the blockchain infrastructure — from the design and implementation of consensus protocols, to the programming layers above them, and the DeFi applications handling billions of dollars — and explore the different ways these systems can—and have—failed under adversarial settings. We will study attacks against real systems, revisit the security and assumptions of widely deployed protocols and applications, and analyze the impact of attacks, scams, and thefts.
